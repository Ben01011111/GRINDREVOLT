F 900 R

The BMW F900 R is a dynamic and aggressive roadster designed 
to deliver thrilling performance and versatility. It features an 
895cc parallel-twin engine that produces 105 hp and 92 Nm of torque,
providing a responsive and engaging ride. With its sharp handling 
and balanced chassis, the F900 R is equally at home on winding roads 
or in city traffic.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 895cc, parallel-twin, liquid-cooled, DOHC (Double Overhead Camshaft).

Power Output: 105 hp (77 kW) @ 8,500 rpm.

Torque: 92 Nm (68 lb-ft) @ 6,500 rpm, offering robust low- to mid-range performance.

Configuration: Electronic fuel injection with dual throttle valves for precise control and efficiency.

Valve System: DOHC with four valves per cylinder, ensuring optimal airflow and combustion efficiency.

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Optimized for efficiency with approximately 22 km/l, making it ideal for both spirited rides and daily commutes.

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by an 895cc parallel-twin engine, producing 105 hp at 8,500 rpm and 92 Nm of torque at 6,500 rpm.


Comfort and Design: Aggressive naked-bike styling with sharp lines and dynamic proportions.


Advanced Technology: Equipped with Dynamic ESA (Electronic Suspension Adjustment) for adaptable handling in various conditions.


Braking and Safety: Dual Brembo front disc brakes and a single rear disc brake offer robust stopping power.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Optimized fuel efficiency of approximately 20 km/l, ensuring a balance of performance and economy.
