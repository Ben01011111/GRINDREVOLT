F 850 GS

The BMW F850 GS is a versatile adventure motorcycle designed 
for riders seeking a blend of performance, comfort, and 
off-road capability. It is powered by an 853cc parallel-twin 
engine that delivers a balanced mix of power and efficiency, 
making it suitable for both highway cruising and rugged trails.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 853cc, water-cooled 4-stroke in-line two-cylinder engine, four valves per cylinder, with dry sump lubrication.

Power Output: 853cc, water-cooled 4-stroke in-line two-cylinder engine, four valves per cylinder, with dry sump lubrication.

Torque: 92 Nm at 6,250 rpm.

Configuration: Electronic fuel injection with a closed-loop 3-way catalytic converter adhering to EU-5 emission standards.

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: 4.2 liters per 100 km (based on WMTC testing).

Key Features:

Chassis and Suspension: Steel bridge-type frame with 43 mm upside-down telescopic fork.


Wheels and Brakes: Dual floating front disc brakes (305 mm) and a rear single disc brake (265 mm) with BMW Motorrad ABS.


Comfort and Customization: Seat height options ranging from 805 mm to 890 mm for different preferences.


Performance: Top speed of over 200 km/h.


Advanced Technology: Features like dynamic ESA (Electronic Suspension Adjustment) and optional connectivity through TFT display.
