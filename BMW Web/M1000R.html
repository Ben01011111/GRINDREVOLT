M 1000 R

The BMW M 1000 R is a performance-oriented naked bike that draws inspiration from BMW's superbike legacy. 
Featuring a 999cc inline-4 engine, the M 1000 R boasts a staggering 205 horsepower at 13,000 rpm, 
making it one of the most powerful naked bikes in its class.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 999cc inline-4, water-cooled, DOHC.

Power Output: 205 hp at 13,500 rpm.

Torque: 113 Nm at 11,000 rpm, ensuring thrilling acceleration.

Configuration: 6-speed gearbox with a slipper clutch, ride-by-wire technology for smooth throttle control.

Valve System: DOHC with variable valve timing, providing a balance of performance across different engine speeds.

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The M 1000 R has powered by a 999cc, inline-4, liquid-cooled, DOHC (Dual Overhead Camshaft) engine.


Comfort and Design: The M 1000 R features an aerodynamic design with a focus on weight reduction and stability, boasting carbon fiber elements to reduce weight.


Advanced Technology: Dynamic Traction Control (DTC) and BMW Motorrad ABS Pro ensure safety and control, especially in high-performance riding scenarios.


Braking and Safety: Features M carbon brakes for maximum stopping power and fully adjustable suspension for precise handling on all surfaces.
