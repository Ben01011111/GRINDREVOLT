NINJA ZX-10RR

Track-Ready Performance, Unmatched Precision

The Kawasaki Ninja ZX-10RR is engineered for the track, offering superior power and precise handling, with a top-tier electronic system that enables riders to control every aspect of their performance. Whether you're at the track or the open road, this bike delivers unparalleled speed, comfort, and cutting-edge technology.


Pricing and Options

The Kawasaki Ninja ZX-10RR is priced at approximately PHP 1,800,000 to PHP 1,900,000, depending on the dealership and specific model year

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 4-stroke, In-Line Four, DOHC, 16-valve, liquid-cooled

Power Output: 214 hp (158.1 kW)

Torque: 82.5 lb-ft (111.8 Nm)

Configuration: In-line four-cylinder

Valve System: DOHC, 16 valves

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Around 16-18 km/L under moderate conditions, but it varies depending on riding style

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The Ninja ZX-10RR is powered by a 998cc, 4-cylinder engine that produces 214 hp, ensuring explosive acceleration and top-tier performance on both the road and track. Its advanced fuel injection system and lightweight construction make it a beast in terms of power-to-weight ratio


Comfort and Design: The bike features a meticulously designed ergonomics setup with a seat height of 32.9 inches, optimized for both track riders and long-distance enthusiasts. Its aerodynamic bodywork and minimalistic design add to the sportiness while providing comfort for daily rides​


Advanced Technology: Equipped with an array of electronic systems such as Kawasaki Traction Control (S-KTRC), Launch Control Mode (KLCM), and Cornering Management Function (KCMF), the ZX-10RR ensures smooth control and exceptional safety. Additionally, the bike features a full-color TFT display and smartphone connectivity via the Rideology app


Braking and Safety: The ZX-10RR comes with Brembo dual semi-floating discs and a KIBS (Kawasaki Intelligent Anti-lock Brake System) that ensures precise braking performance. Whether on tight corners or hard stops, it offers superior stability and control​


Eco-Friendly Innovations: The Ninja ZX-10RR's fuel management system includes an Economical Riding Indicator, which helps optimize fuel usage for better efficiency. Its electronic throttle system also reduces emissions, contributing to a cleaner environment
