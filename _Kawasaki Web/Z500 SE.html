Z500 SE

The Kawasaki Z500 SE is a striking and versatile middleweight naked bike designed 
to deliver both thrilling performance and everyday usability. Powered by a 499cc 
parallel twin engine, it strikes a perfect balance between power and efficiency,
making it ideal for both city commuting and weekend escapes.

Z500 SE

The Z500 SE: Where Power Meets Precision

The Kawasaki Z500 SE offers a dynamic, lightweight design combined with a powerful twin-cylinder engine, delivering an exhilarating riding experience. Whether you're navigating the city streets or hitting open roads, this bike provides a balanced blend of performance and handling, giving riders the confidence they need for every ride.


Pricing and Options

The Kawasaki Z500 SE is expected to be priced around PHP 290,000 - PHP 370,000 approximately, depending on the model year, features, and dealership.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: Liquid-cooled, 4-stroke, parallel twin

Power Output: Approximately 47 hp (35 kW) @ 8,500 rpm

Torque: 45 Nm @ 6,500 rpm

Configuration: Parallel twin

Valve System: DOHC (Dual Overhead Cam), 8 valves

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Around 25-30 km/L, depending on riding conditions

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The 499cc parallel twin engine is designed to offer responsive acceleration and smooth performance, ideal for both city commuting and longer rides.


Comfort and Design: The Z500 SE is designed with an ergonomic, rider-friendly posture to ensure comfort during long rides.


Advanced Technology: Equipped with modern electronics, including an advanced digital instrument cluster for clear, quick data access.


Braking and Safety: The Z500 SE is also equipped with a high-performance suspension system, ensuring a smooth and stable ride on various terrains.
