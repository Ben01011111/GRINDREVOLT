VULCAN S

Ride the Streets with Confidence and Power.

The Kawasaki Vulcan S delivers the perfect blend of power, comfort, and style. Built for those who seek adventure with a custom fit, this cruiser offers a responsive engine and ergonomic design for a smooth, enjoyable ride. Whether you're navigating city streets or cruising down the highway, the Vulcan S is your reliable companion, bringing out your inner rider.


Pricing and Options

The price of the Kawasaki Vulcan S in the Philippines generally ranges from ₱399,000 to ₱420,000, depending on the dealer and any ongoing promotions.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: Parallel-twin, liquid-cooled

Power Output: 61 horsepower (45.5 kW) @ 7,500 rpm

Torque: 63.7 Nm @ 6,600 rpm

Configuration: 649 cc

Valve System: DOHC, 8 valves

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 17 km/L (varies based on riding conditions)

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The 649cc parallel-twin engine offers a perfect balance of power and efficiency, with 61 horsepower to deliver a smooth ride. The engine is paired with a 6-speed transmission, allowing seamless shifting and consistent power output for various riding conditions.


Comfort and Design: The Vulcan S features an adjustable Ergo-Fit system, offering three seat options, footpeg adjustments, and handlebar positions to suit a wide range of riders. This customization ensures an optimal riding position, making longer rides more comfortable and reducing fatigue. The sleek cruiser design is both modern and bold, making a statement on the road.


Advanced Technology: The Vulcan S is equipped with modern technology such as a digital LCD display, showing vital riding information like speed, gear position, fuel level, and more. The LED tail light enhances visibility for safer night rides, and the assist-and-slipper clutch provides smooth shifting.


Braking and Safety: Featuring a 300mm front disc brake and a 250mm rear disc brake, the Vulcan S ensures reliable stopping power. Coupled with ABS (Anti-lock Braking System) for improved safety, it provides stability and control in various road conditions, especially in challenging weather.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Designed with fuel efficiency in mind, the Vulcan S achieves around 17 km/L, reducing the frequency of fuel stops while minimizing environmental impact. The engine is optimized to meet stringent emission standards, contributing to a cleaner environment.
