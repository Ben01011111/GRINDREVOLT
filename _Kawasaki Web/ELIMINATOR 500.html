ELIMINATOR 500

Ride the Road, Own the Journey

The Kawasaki Eliminator 500 offers an accessible and comfortable ride, designed for those who crave both style and power on the road.


Pricing and Options

The price of the Kawasaki Eliminator 500 ranges from P360,000 to P380,000, depending on the variant

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 4-Stroke Parallel Twin, DOHC, 8 valves, Liquid Cooled

Power Output: 52 hp (38.8 kW)

Torque: 42.6 Nm

Configuration: Parallel Twin

Valve System: DOHC, 8 valves

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Around 31 km/l (74 mpg)

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by a 451cc parallel twin engine, the Eliminator delivers a solid 52 hp, providing ample power for both city commutes and longer highway rides. The smooth, responsive performance is perfect for riders seeking an agile but powerful machine.


Comfort and Design: The low seat height of 735 mm provides excellent accessibility, while its lightweight frame enhances comfort, making it suitable for a wide range of riders. Its modern cruiser aesthetic is complemented by its clean lines and relaxed riding position.


Advanced Technology: Equipped with modern features like a fuel-injected system, LED lighting, and a USB charging port, the Eliminator ensures convenience on the go. Its keyless ignition and phone connectivity elevate the riding experience.


Braking and Safety: The bike includes ABS for added safety, with reliable single-disc brakes on both the front and rear for confident stopping power. A slipper clutch ensures smoother downshifts and improves overall rideability.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: The efficient fuel consumption, combined with low emissions, makes this bike a great option for environmentally-conscious riders who still want a powerful cruiser.
