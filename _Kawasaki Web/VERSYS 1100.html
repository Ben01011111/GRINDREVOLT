VERSYS 1100

Unleash Adventure, Command the Road.

The Kawasaki Versys 1100 is designed for riders who crave thrilling performance without compromising on comfort. Whether you’re on a winding mountain road or cruising on highways, this bike offers a blend of power, comfort, and cutting-edge tech to make every journey exhilarating.


Pricing and Options

The Kawasaki Versys 1100's price is approximately PHP 1,099,000 based on international pricing​

Engine Specification

Engine Type: Liquid-cooled, 4-stroke, DOHC, in-line 4-cylinder

Power Output: 135 PS (133 hp) @ 9,500 rpm

Torque: 102 Nm @ 7,500 rpm

Configuration: In-line 4-cylinder

Valve System: DOHC, 16 valves

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Advanced ECU programming provides excellent fuel efficiency with a 21-liter fuel tank for extended touring

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The 1,099cc in-line 4 engine boosts power to 135 PS, delivering exceptional mid-range torque and smooth power across all RPMs. It ensures a responsive ride whether cruising or tackling twists


Comfort and Design: Its sporty yet comfortable design features an adjustable windscreen, offering optimal wind protection. The upright riding position ensures control and comfort for long-distance touring​


Advanced Technology: With a refined quickshifter, adaptive suspension (KECS with Showa’s Skyhook Technology), and voice-controlled Rideology, the Versys 1100 integrates cutting-edge technology to enhance ride quality


Braking and Safety: Features include cornering lights, KTRC (Kawasaki Traction Control), and a robust IMU-enhanced electronics package for excellent stability and safety during challenging rides


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Optimized fuel efficiency thanks to advanced engine management, contributing to a greener and more sustainable ride
