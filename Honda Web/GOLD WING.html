GOLD WING

The Honda Gold Wing is a motorcycle built for touring, equipped with the latest technology to ensure both comfort 
and performance on long-distance rides. This bike is perfect for riders who seek to travel in comfort and style 
and enjoying the thrills of the open road.

the Gold Wing’s primary appeal to those seeking unmatched comfort and performance on long journeys, making it a top choice for serious touring enthusiasts.


Pricing and Options

The price of the Honda Gold Wing in the Philippines generally ranges from ₱1,100,000 to ₱1,500,000 depending on the model and any optional upgrades or packages.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 1,833cc liquid-cooled horizontally opposed 6-cylinder engine

Power Output: Around 125 horsepower

Torque: 170 Nm

Configuration: 6-speed transmission with shaft drive

Valve System: SOHC, four valves per cylinder

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 16-20 km/l, depending on riding conditions

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The 6-cylinder engine offers plenty of power while maintaining smooth, refined handling.


Comfort and Design: Features include a plush seat, a high-tech suspension system, adjustable heated grips, and a smooth, quiet ride with a multi-adjustable windscreen.


Advanced Technology: Includes a full-color TFT display, Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, navigation system, and integrated Bluetooth for seamless connectivity.


Braking and Safety: Honda’s advanced safety features, including the Anti-lock Braking System (ABS), traction control, and cornering capabilities.


Infotainment System: Built-in infotainment system with touch control, navigation, and an easy-to-read display.
