CRF AFRICA TWIN ADVENTURE SPORTS 

The Honda CRF Africa Twin Adventure Sports is an advanced version of the classic Africa Twin, 
designed to handle the toughest terrains while offering comfort for long-distance touring. 
With a larger fuel tank, improved suspension, and advanced electronics, 
it stands as one of the top choices for adventure riders.

Pricing and Options

Estimated Price Range: ₱950,000 to ₱1,150,000, depending on dealer pricing, model year, and optional accessories.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 1084cc, liquid-cooled, parallel-twin

Power Output: 102 hp (76 kW) @ 7,500 rpm

Torque: 105 Nm @ 6,250 rpm

Configuration: Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI) with ride-by-wire throttle for smoother throttle response.

Valve System: Unicam, DOHC (Dual Overhead Camshaft)

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 18 km/l (5.6 l/100 km), depending on riding conditions.

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The Africa Twin Adventure Sports is powered by a 1084cc parallel-twin engine, offering robust power and torque, ideal for off-road and highway performance.


Comfort and Design: Comfortable Suspension: The model features long-travel suspension with 45mm Showa front forks and a Showa rear shock, ensuring top-notch comfort on rough trails.


Advanced Technology: Dual Clutch Transmission (DCT): Optional for seamless automatic shifting or manual control, offering an easier ride in challenging conditions.


Braking and Safety: Braking: Dual 310mm front discs with twin-piston calipers and a 256mm rear disc for balanced braking.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: The bike is equipped with a fuel-efficient system and is designed to comply with Euro 5 emissions regulations, making it both eco-friendly and performance-driven.
