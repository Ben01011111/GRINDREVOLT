CL500

The Honda CL500 is a stylish and versatile motorcycle that blends retro design with modern technology. 
It is designed to offer a smooth ride on both paved roads and light off-road terrains, 
combining the practicality of a street bike with the adventure spirit of a scrambler.

Pricing and Options

The Honda CL500 is expected to be priced in the Philippines around ₱350,000 to ₱420,000, depending on the dealer and any optional accessories.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 471cc liquid-cooled parallel-twin engine.

Power Output: Approximately 47 horsepower.

Torque: Around 43.2 Nm (31.9 lb-ft) at 6,000 rpm.

Configuration: The engine uses a DOHC (Dual Overhead Cam) configuration, providing a balance of power and efficiency.

Valve System: 4 valves per cylinder with a DOHC design, ensuring smooth performance and efficient combustion.

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: The CL500 delivers a fuel economy of approximately 25-30 km/l depending on riding conditions and usage.

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by a 471cc liquid-cooled parallel-twin engine, offering a balance between everyday usability and sufficient power for weekend adventures.


Comfort and Design: Features a scrambler-inspired design, with high-mounted exhausts and a minimalist retro aesthetic.


Advanced Technology: Comes equipped with LED lighting and a digital instrument cluster for modern convenience.


Versatility: The dual-purpose tires are optimized for both on-road and light off-road handling, making the CL500 a great option for riders who enjoy mixed-terrain adventures.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: With a focus on daily usability, the CL500 is designed for efficiency, with good fuel economy that supports long-distance rides.
