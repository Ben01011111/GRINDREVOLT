Engine Specification

Engine Type: 649cc DOHC Inline-4, liquid-cooled

Power Output: 93 hp (69 kW) @ 12,000 rpm

Torque: 64 Nm (47 lb-ft) @ 8,500 rpm

Configuration: 4-stroke, inline-four engine

Valve System: DOHC (Dual Overhead Camshaft), 16 valves

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 22-25 km/l, depending on riding conditions.

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The CBR650R is powered by a 649cc DOHC Inline-4 engine, delivering 93 hp and 64 Nm of torque, providing a smooth, high-revving engine performance ideal for both city commuting and aggressive cornering.


Comfort and Design: Aggressive styling inspired by Honda's larger sportbikes, featuring sharp lines, a sleek headlight design, and aerodynamic bodywork for improved performance at high speeds.


Advanced Technology: Honda Selectable Torque Control (HSTC): A system that helps the rider maintain control by reducing the risk of rear wheel slip on slippery surfaces.


Braking and Safety: Dual front disc brakes and a single rear disc brake, with ABS (Anti-lock Braking System) for enhanced braking safety and stability.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: The bike offers good fuel efficiency (around 22-25 km/l), thanks to its efficient inline-four engine and lightweight chassis, ideal for long-distance touring or daily commuting.
