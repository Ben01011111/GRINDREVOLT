Pricing and Options

Price in the Philippines: Estimated around ₱650,000 - ₱700,000 depending on variant and dealer promotions.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 1084cc, liquid-cooled, parallel-twin.

Power Output: Approximately 101 hp (75 kW).

Torque: 105 Nm (77.4 lb-ft) at 6250 rpm.

Configuration: 6-speed transmission with a choice of standard or Dual Clutch Transmission (DCT) for smoother shifting.

Valve System: DOHC (Dual Overhead Camshaft), offering precise valve timing and efficiency.

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Around 20-22 km/l, depending on riding conditions.

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The 1084cc parallel-twin engine offers a robust 101 hp and 105 Nm of torque, delivering smooth power for both on-road and off-road adventures.


Comfort and Design: The Africa Twin has a comfortable upright seating position, suitable for long-distance touring.


Advanced Technology: 7-inch TFT Display: Provides clear, customizable information, including navigation, riding modes, and trip data.


Braking and Safety: Honda Selectable Torque Control (HSTC): This system helps maintain traction by controlling rear-wheel slip in challenging conditions.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Large Fuel Tank (24.8L): Ensures extended range for long off-road journeys, reducing the need for frequent refueling.


Off-road Capabilities: Advanced Traction Control: Helps prevent wheel spin and ensures stability, especially on loose or slippery surfaces.
