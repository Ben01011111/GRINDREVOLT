REBEL 1100

The Honda Rebel 1100 combines classic cruiser design with modern performance 
and technology, appealing to riders looking for both style and convenience.

REBEL 1100

Unleash the Rebel

The Rebel 1100 tagline emphasizes its ability to break away from the conventional cruiser design while offering superior performance.


Pricing and Options

The Rebel 1100's price varies by market, but in the Philippines, it typically starts at around ₱530,000 to ₱600,000, depending on the specific variant and dealer offerings.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 1,084cc Parallel-Twin

Power Output: 87 horsepower

Torque:91 Nm (67 lb-ft) at 6,000 rpm

Configuration: Liquid-cooled, four-cylinder V4

Valve System: DOHC (Dual Overhead Camshaft)

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Rear-cylinder deactivation technology reduces fuel consumption during light throttle or idle conditions

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The Rebel 1100 is powered by a robust 1,084cc engine that delivers impressive power and torque. It's paired with a 6-speed gearbox or optional DCT (Dual-Clutch Transmission) for a smooth, hassle-free ride.


Comfort and Design: The low seat height and minimalist styling are designed for both comfort and accessibility. The handlebar positions ensure a relaxed riding posture, ideal for long-distance cruises.


Advanced Technology: The bike is equipped with Honda Selectable Torque Control (HSTC), three riding modes, and a 7-inch TFT display. It provides customization options for both performance and safety, including traction control and throttle response adjustments.


Braking and Safety: Features a Honda's Pro-Link rear suspension system and Brembo brakes for smooth and responsive handling. This is paired with a 43mm front fork for excellent stability.


Customization Options: Honda offers multiple customization options to modify the Rebel 1100's aesthetic to your personal preference, from custom seats to exhaust upgrades.
