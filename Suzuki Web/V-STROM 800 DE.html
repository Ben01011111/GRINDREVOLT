Engine Specification

Engine Type: Twin-cylinder, 4-stroke, liquid-cooled, DOHC engine

Power Output: 83 horsepower (62 kW) at 8500 rpm.

Torque: 78 Nm at 6800 rpm.

Configuration: DOHC with a compression ratio of 12.8:1.

Valve System: Double overhead camshaft (DOHC) with 4 valves per cylinder.

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 22.7 km/L (4.4 L/100 km) based on WMTC standards​

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Equipped with a 776cc parallel-twin DOHC engine, delivering 84.3 hp @ 8,500 rpm and 78 Nm torque @ 6,800 rpm.


Comfort and Design: Offers an 8.7-inch ground clearance, and long-travel suspension for tackling uneven terrain.


Advanced Technology: Includes Suzuki’s Drive Mode Selector (3 modes) and Suzuki Traction Control System with Gravel Mode.


Braking and Safety: Dual Nissin front disc brakes and single rear brake provide reliable stopping power.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Designed for fuel efficiency without compromising power, offering a practical range for adventure touring.
