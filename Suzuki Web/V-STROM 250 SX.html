V-STROM 250 SX

The Suzuki V-Strom 250SX is a versatile adventure motorcycle designed for lightweight touring 
and everyday commuting. Its compact size and robust features make it suitable for a variety 
of terrains, from city streets to light off-road trails.

Pricing and Options

The Suzuki V-Strom 250 SX is priced in the Philippines at ₱229,000. This affordable adventure bike offers versatility and practicality for riders seeking on-road and light off-road capabilities​

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 248cc single-cylinder, four-stroke, liquid-cooled, SOHC.

Power Output: 25 HP at 8,000 RPM.

Torque: 23.4 Nm at 6,500 RPM.

Configuration: Liquid-cooled, four-cylinder V4

Valve System: Desmodromic valve-less system, reducing maintenance requirements

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Rear-cylinder deactivation technology reduces fuel consumption during light throttle or idle conditions

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by a 248cc parallel-twin engine, producing 24.7 hp at 8,000 rpm and 23.4 Nm of torque at 6,500 rpm.


Comfort and Design: Upright seating position with a comfortable seat height of 800mm, suitable for long rides and varied rider heights.


Advanced Technology: Features a rugged design inspired by larger V-Strom models, including spoke-style wheels and a skid plate.


Braking and Safety: Designed with rear luggage mounts for carrying top boxes or panniers, enhancing its touring functionality.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Fuel tank capacity of 17 liters, offering extended range for long-distance adventures.
