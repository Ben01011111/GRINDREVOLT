GSX-R1000R

Unleash the Power, Dominate the Track.

The Suzuki GSX-R1000R is a high-performance supersport motorcycle designed for riders who demand precision, power, and cutting-edge technology. Powered by a 999cc inline-4 engine, the GSX-R1000R offers exceptional acceleration, top-tier handling, and incredible braking performance, making it a true track machine.


Pricing and Options

The Suzuki GSX-R1000R is priced at ₱1,300,000 in the Philippines, reflecting its high-performance engineering, advanced features, and track-ready capabilities.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 999.8cc Liquid-cooled, 4-stroke, DOHC, inline-4

Power Output: 199 hp (148 kW) @ 13,200 rpm

Torque: 117.6 Nm @ 10,800 rpm

Configuration: Suzuki Dual Throttle Valve (SDTV) electronic fuel injection

Valve System: 6-speed constant mesh

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: The GSX-R1000R is powered by a 999.8cc inline-4 engine, producing 199 horsepower and 117.6 Nm of torque. It’s a race-bred machine designed for ultimate performance on both the track and the road, with an efficient fuel system to maximize fuel efficiency.

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: 999cc, inline-4 engine, 202 hp, 118.8 Nm of torque, and a top speed of over 186 mph.


Comfort and Design: Aerodynamic bodywork, lightweight chassis, and ergonomic seating for improved rider comfort on long rides.


Advanced Technology: Suzuki Intelligent Ride System (SIRS), Ride-by-Wire throttle, three-mode traction control, and more for optimized control.


Braking and Safety: Brembo brakes, cornering ABS, and advanced suspension system ensure unmatched stopping power and control.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Compliant with Euro 5 emissions standards, balancing power and environmental responsibility.
