Pricing and Options

The Suzuki GSX-S1000GT is priced at approximately ₱925,600 to ₱999,000 in the Philippines, depending on the variant

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 999cc, Inline-4, liquid-cooled, DOHC

Power Output: 150 hp (112 kW) @ 11,000 rpm

Torque: 106 Nm @ 9,250 rpm

Configuration: 4-cylinder, 4-stroke engine, with a 6-speed gearbox and a smooth power delivery designed for high-speed touring.

Valve System: DOHC (Double Overhead Camshaft)

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 16 km/l (6.25 liters per 100 km), optimized for both performance and touring range.

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by a 999cc inline-four engine that delivers 150 hp at 11,000 rpm and 106 Nm of torque at 9,250 rpm.


Comfort and Design: Ergonomic design with a rider-friendly, adjustable seat and a windscreen optimized for long-distance comfort.


Advanced Technology: Suzuki Intelligent Ride System (SIRS) includes modes for better control and performance, such as Suzuki Drive Mode Selector (SDMS), traction control, and electronic throttle valves.


Braking and Safety: Brembo brake components for outstanding stopping power, providing confidence when braking at high speeds or in tricky conditions.


Connectivity and Customization: Smartphone integration through Bluetooth for navigation, music, and call management, allowing riders to stay connected on the go.
