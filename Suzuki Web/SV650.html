SV650

The Suzuki SV650 is a versatile and stylish middleweight motorcycle designed 
for both novice and experienced riders. Known for its lightweight frame, 
responsive handling, and reliable performance, the SV650 offers a perfect
balance of practicality and fun. It is well-suited for daily commutes, 
spirited weekend rides, and light touring.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 645cc, 4-stroke, liquid-cooled, DOHC, 90° V-twin.

Power Output: Approximately 73 hp (54 kW) @ 8,500 rpm.

Torque: 64 Nm (47 lb-ft) @ 8,100 rpm.

Configuration: Fuel-injected V-twin engine with precise throttle response.

Valve System: DOHC with four valves per cylinder.

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Around 24 km/l (56 mpg), making it economical for daily use.

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Equipped with a 645cc V-twin engine delivering 73 hp and 64 Nm of torque, offering a balance of power and efficiency.


Comfort and Design: A low seat height of 785mm makes it accessible for a wide range of riders.


Advanced Technology: The Suzuki Easy Start System simplifies engine start-ups with just one button press.


Braking and Safety: Low RPM Assist feature prevents stalling during low-speed maneuvers, enhancing usability.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Excellent mileage of around 24 km/l, making it a cost-effective option for commuters and enthusiasts alike.
