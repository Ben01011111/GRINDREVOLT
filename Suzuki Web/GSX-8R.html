GSX-8R

The Suzuki GSX-8R is a middleweight sportbike that was introduced 
to offer a balance between performance, handling, and usability, 
catering to both new and experienced riders. With its sleek design 
and refined engineering, it serves as an exciting option for those 
looking to upgrade from entry-level bikes or for enthusiasts seeking 
an agile, spirited ride.

Pricing and Options

the price of the Suzuki GSX-8R is typically around ₱500,000 to ₱550,000, though this can vary depending on the dealer, any ongoing promotions, and additional costs such as taxes or registration fees.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 776cc liquid-cooled, parallel-twin

Power Output: 82 hp @ 8,500 rpm

Torque: 78 Nm @ 6,800 rpm

Configuration: Inline 2-cylinder, parallel-twin

Valve System: DOHC (Double Overhead Camshaft) with 4 valves per cylinder

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 18-20 km/L, depending on riding conditions

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The GSX-8R is powered by an 776cc parallel-twin engine that delivers a good mix of power and torque for a balanced ride.


Comfort and Design: The GSX-8R has a sporty, aggressive look, drawing design cues from Suzuki’s larger GSX-R series. Its aerodynamic fairings, sharp lines, and sleek headlamp design emphasize its race-inspired aesthetics.


Advanced Technology: The motorcycle includes modern electronics, such as a ride-by-wire throttle system, traction control, and multiple riding modes, enhancing safety and performance. These features help maintain traction during acceleration and provide riders with better control.


Braking and Safety: The GSX-8R comes equipped with powerful twin disc brakes at the front and a single disc brake at the rear, providing excellent stopping power and control. The inclusion of ABS (Anti-lock Braking System) is standard, improving braking performance in various conditions.


Chassis and Suspension: The GSX-8R features a lightweight, aluminum twin-spar frame for enhanced agility and handling, ensuring excellent cornering capabilities and stability at higher speeds.
