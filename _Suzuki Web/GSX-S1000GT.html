GSX-S1000GT

The Suzuki GSX-S1000GT is a sport-touring motorcycle that blends high-performance 
capabilities with everyday usability. Powered by the same 999cc, 4-cylinder engine 
found in the GSX-R1000, the GSX-S1000GT offers thrilling acceleration, precise handling, 
and a smooth, powerful ride.

GSX-S1000GT

Where Performance Meets Comfort, Ready for Any Journey.

The Suzuki GSX-S1000GT is a sport-touring motorcycle designed to deliver a thrilling riding experience, combining high-performance features with exceptional comfort for long-distance journeys.


Pricing and Options

The Suzuki GSX-S1000GT is priced starting at approximately PHP 850,000 to PHP 950,000, depending on the dealership and location.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 999cc, 4-stroke, liquid-cooled, inline-4 engine

Power Output: Approximately 150 horsepower (112 kW) at 10,000 rpm

Torque: Approximately 106 Nm at 9,250 rpm

Configuration: Inline-4

Valve System: DOHC (Dual Overhead Cam)

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Estimated around 17-18 km/l depending on riding conditions

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: 999cc, inline-4 engine, 150 hp, 106 Nm of torque, delivering smooth and responsive power for an exciting ride.


Comfort and Design: Relaxed and ergonomic riding position, adjustable windshield, and comfortable seat for long-distance touring.


Advanced Technology: Suzuki Intelligent Ride System (SIRS), cruise control, quick-shifter, three-mode traction control, and a fully digital TFT display for enhanced rider experience.


Braking and Safety: Dual Brembo front brakes, cornering ABS, and advanced suspension for improved handling and stability.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Compliant with Euro 5 emissions standards, offering a balance of performance and environmental responsibility.
