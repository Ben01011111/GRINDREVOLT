GSX-8S

Bold Performance, Everyday Thrills.

The Suzuki GSX-8S is a dynamic and versatile naked sportbike designed for riders who crave excitement and agility on every ride. With its 776cc parallel-twin engine, the GSX-8S delivers a perfect balance of power and efficiency, making it ideal for both city commutes and spirited weekend rides.


Pricing and Options

the Suzuki GSX-8S is priced around ₱549,000. The price may vary depending on the dealer, location, and any ongoing promotions or offers.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 4-stroke, parallel-twin, DOHC (Dual Overhead Cam)

Power Output: 82.1 hp (61 kW) @ 8,500 rpm

Torque: 78 Nm @ 6,800 rpm

Configuration: Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI)

Valve System: DOHC, 4 valves per cylinder

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approx. 22 km/L (depending on riding conditions)

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The GSX-8S is powered by a 776cc parallel-twin engine that offers 82.1 horsepower and 78 Nm of torque, providing an exciting yet manageable riding experience. Fuel-efficient and responsive, it’s a perfect blend of power and economy for both city and highway riding.


Comfort and Design: With an upright, ergonomic seating posture and sleek styling, the GSX-8S balances comfort with cutting-edge aesthetics. The low seat height ensures accessibility, while the modern digital display and LED lights complete the bike’s futuristic look.


Advanced Technology: The GSX-8S is packed with advanced tech like Ride-by-Wire, adjustable traction control, and a one-touch start system for convenience. Bosch ABS enhances braking safety, ensuring greater control during emergency situations.


Braking and Safety: The GSX-8S features robust braking with dual front discs and a rear disc brake, complemented by Bosch ABS for enhanced control. The suspension system offers a smooth, comfortable ride while the traction control system ensures stability, particularly during challenging conditions.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Designed with sustainability in mind, the GSX-8S offers great fuel efficiency and complies with Euro 5 emission standards. The EFI system ensures optimal fuel use and minimal environmental impact, making it an eco-friendly choice for riders.
