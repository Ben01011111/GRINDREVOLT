HAYABUSA

The Suzuki Hayabusa is a renowned high-performance sportbike, famous for its powerful engine,
 aerodynamic design, and impressive top speed. Known as one of the fastest motorcycles in the world, 
 the Hayabusa has a legacy in the world of motorcycling, often being the benchmark for speed and technology.

HAYABUSA

The King of Speed

The Hayabusa’s long-standing reputation as one of the fastest motorcycles in the world underscores its identity as the undisputed leader in speed and performance.


Pricing and Options

The price in the Philippines for the Suzuki Hayabusa typically ranges from ₱1,100,000 to ₱1,250,000, depending on the model year and dealer promotions.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 1,340cc, inline 4-cylinder, DOHC, liquid-cooled

Power Output: 190 hp (141 kW) at 9,700 rpm

Torque: 150 Nm (110 lb-ft) at 7,000 rpm

Configuration: 6-speed constant mesh

Valve System: DOHC (Double Overhead Camshaft)

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The Hayabusa is known for its impressive top speed and acceleration, with a smooth and powerful engine that delivers a thrilling riding experience.


Comfort and Design: Its comfortable ergonomics make it suitable for both high-speed track days and long touring rides, providing a balance between speed and comfort.


Advanced Technology: Traction Control System (S-TCS): Helps maintain stability during acceleration, particularly in difficult road conditions.


Braking and Safety: KYB suspension ensures a smooth ride and effective handling during high-speed riding or cornering.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Despite being a high-performance machine, the Hayabusa manages a reasonable fuel efficiency of around 12–14 km/l, making it practical for long-distance touring as well as spirited riding.
