1100 Sport Pro

Embrace the ride with style and power—where tradition meets innovation.

This modern iteration of Ducati's Scrambler line blends classic charm with cutting-edge technology, making it a standout choice for motorcycle enthusiasts. Whether you're on an urban commute or exploring rugged landscapes, the 1100 Sport Pro is built to handle it all.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 1,079cc Desmodromic L-Twin, air-cooled

Power Output: 86 hp (63 kW) @ 7,500 rpm

Torque: 65 lb-ft (88 Nm) @ 4,750 rpm

Configuration: Bosch electronic fuel injection with 53 mm throttle bodies and ride-by-wire technology, for precise throttle control and smooth power delivery.

Valve System: Desmodromic valve-less system, which helps to reduce maintenance requirements while providing optimal performance.

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 17.9 km/l (5.6 liters per 100 km), showcasing a balance between power and fuel economy.

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by a 937cc Testastretta 11° twin-cylinder engine producing 111 hp at 9,250 rpm and 93 Nm torque at 6,500 rpm.


Comfort and Design: Lightweight aluminum alloy frame and ergonomic enhancements prioritize rider comfort and handling.


Advanced Technology: Equipped with a 4.3-inch TFT display for easy access to riding modes, system settings, and important data while on the move.


Braking and Safety: Fitted with Pirelli Diablo Rosso IV tires to ensure enhanced grip and traction, even in challenging riding conditions.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Fuel efficiency of 18.87 km/l, offering a balance of sporty performance with an economical fuel consumption rate.
