TÉNÉRÉ 700

Conquer Every Terrain with Confidence

The Yamaha Ténéré 700 is a versatile adventure bike engineered for endurance and performance, offering both ruggedness and comfort for long rides through challenging landscapes.


Pricing and Options

The Yamaha Ténéré 700 is priced at ₱669,000 in the Philippines

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 689cc, liquid-cooled, 4-stroke, DOHC, parallel twin

Power Output: 72 hp (53.5 kW) @ 9,000 rpm

Torque: 68 Nm @ 6,500 rpm

Configuration: Parallel twin

Valve System: DOHC (Double Overhead Camshaft)

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 20-22 km/L depending on riding conditions

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by a 689cc parallel-twin engine, the Ténéré 700 delivers impressive power and torque, ensuring smooth acceleration and stability on both highways and rugged trails. The 6-speed transmission complements the engine’s performance for superior control in any terrain.


Comfort and Design: With a height-adjustable seat and ergonomic design, the Ténéré 700 ensures comfort for long-distance riders. The lightweight tubular frame and long travel suspension offer exceptional handling on diverse surfaces, while the compact body enhances maneuverability.


Advanced Technology: The Ténéré 700 is equipped with advanced tech like ABS and traction control to enhance rider safety. The digital instrument cluster provides real-time data, and the adjustable windscreen offers added comfort against wind resistance.


Braking and Safety: Features a reliable disc brake system, with ABS for enhanced control during off-road adventures. The Ténéré 700 also incorporates safety tech like traction control, ensuring stability on slippery or uneven ground.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: The efficient engine and design are Euro-compliant, offering improved fuel economy and reduced emissions for eco-conscious riders.
