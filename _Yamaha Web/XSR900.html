XSR900

Old-School Looks, New-School Performance

The Yamaha XSR900 blends nostalgic design with modern performance. Its 890cc triple engine offers thrilling acceleration, enhanced by a sophisticated suite of rider aids, including traction control and a quick-shift system. With its advanced aluminum frame, lightweight wheels, and striking classic styling, it’s a bike that performs as well as it looks.


Pricing and Options

The 2024 Yamaha XSR900 is priced at ₱729,000 in the Philippines

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 890cc Crossplane Crankshaft Triple, liquid-cooled, DOHC, fuel-injected (YCC-T)

Power Output: 119 hp (87.5 kW) at 10,000 rpm

Torque: 93 Nm at 7,000 rpm

Configuration: Inline 3-cylinder

Valve System: DOHC

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 20 km/L (47 mpg)

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The 890cc CP3 engine delivers strong, linear torque, enhanced by a quick-shift system and an updated exhaust for a more responsive, smoother ride


Comfort and Design: Featuring a sleek design with a sculpted fuel tank and a minimalist look, the bike's seating and ergonomics ensure both comfort and an aggressive stance​


Advanced Technology: A six-axis IMU, ride-by-wire throttle, and selectable power modes elevate the riding experience, providing excellent control and adaptability


Braking and Safety: Equipped with Brembo radial calipers and a sophisticated braking system with lean-sensitive ABS, it offers precise braking power and enhanced stability​


Eco-Friendly Innovations: The engine's direct-plated cylinders and advanced intake/exhaust systems improve fuel efficiency while reducing emissions​
