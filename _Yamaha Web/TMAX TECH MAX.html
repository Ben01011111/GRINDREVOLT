TMAX TECH MAX

The Ultimate Sports Scooter

The TMAX Tech MAX blends sporty performance with touring comfort. Powered by a 561cc engine, it offers an exhilarating 47.6 hp for swift acceleration and confident handling. Whether navigating through tight city streets or cruising on highways, its refined design delivers both comfort and performance.


Pricing and Options

The Yamaha TMAX Tech MAX is priced at ₱849,000 in the Philippines

Engine Specification

Engine Type: Liquid-cooled, 4-stroke, DOHC, parallel twin-cylinder

Power Output: 47.6 HP (35 kW) at 7,500 rpm

Torque: 56 Nm at 5,250 rpm

Configuration: Parallel twin-cylinder

Valve System: DOHC (Dual Overhead Cam)

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 25-30 km/l under normal riding conditions

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Liquid-cooled 561cc parallel twin engine offering impressive power and torque. With selectable riding modes (Touring and Sport), riders can tailor the bike’s responsiveness to their needs. The traction control system ensures optimal stability in challenging conditions


Comfort and Design: Premium seat design and adjustable ergonomics make long rides a breeze. The dual LED headlights provide excellent visibility, and the overall build is crafted for both performance and comfort


Advanced Technology: Features include cruise control, heated grips, and a sophisticated TFT display that shows vital information including fuel consumption, trip data, and ride modes. The bike also boasts an anti-lock braking system (ABS) and traction control


Braking and Safety: Dual disc brakes at the front with ABS provide enhanced stopping power, while the stability of the ride is further supported by the advanced traction control system


Eco-Friendly Innovations: The TMAX Tech MAX incorporates advanced environmental technology to meet emission standards while still delivering high performance. Its fuel efficiency helps reduce overall environmental impact
