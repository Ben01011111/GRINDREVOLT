TMAX

Exhilarating Performance, Everyday Comfort

The Yamaha TMAX redefines the maxi-scooter category with its dynamic powertrain and comfort-driven design. Perfect for both the commuter and the leisure rider, the TMAX delivers both thrilling acceleration and superior handling for urban and highway adventures alike.


Pricing and Options

The Yamaha TMAX is priced at approximately ₱779,000 in the Philippines

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 562cc, Liquid-cooled, 4-stroke, DOHC, 4-Valve

Power Output: 47 hp @ 7,500 rpm

Torque: 55.7 Nm @ 5,250 rpm

Configuration: Parallel twin

Valve System: DOHC, 4 valves per cylinder

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 25-28 km/l

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The 562cc engine delivers 47 hp and 55.7 Nm of torque, paired with a CVT system for smooth acceleration and a responsive ride.


Comfort and Design: Its lightweight chassis, ergonomic seat, and advanced suspension system ensure a smooth, stable ride, with a seat height of 800mm to accommodate a wide range of riders.


Advanced Technology: The TMAX features a large, connected TFT screen with MyRide-Link for smartphone connectivity, along with LED lighting and adjustable headlights.


Braking and Safety: Dual disc brakes with ABS enhance stopping power and safety, while the scooter also includes a robust immobilizer system.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: With an efficient fuel consumption rate and a cleaner engine, the TMAX offers excellent fuel economy, achieving up to 28 km/l.
