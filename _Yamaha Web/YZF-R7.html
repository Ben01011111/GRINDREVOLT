YZF-R7

Agility and Performance Combined

The Yamaha YZF-R7 offers a dynamic riding experience with a perfect blend of lightweight design and powerful performance. Its 689cc engine delivers impressive power and torque, making it ideal for both spirited street riding and track days.


Pricing and Options

The Yamaha YZF-R7 2024 is priced at approximately ₱600,000 in the Philippines

Engine Specification

Engine Type: Liquid-cooled, 4-stroke, DOHC inline twin-cylinder; 4-valves per cylinder

Power Output: 72 hp (53.6 kW) at 8,750 rpm

Torque: 67 Nm at 6,500 rpm

Configuration: Inline twin-cylinder, 689cc

Valve System: DOHC (Double Overhead Camshaft), 4 valves per cylinder

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 58 MPG (miles per gallon)​

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by a 689cc, inline twin-cylinder engine, the YZF-R7 produces 72 hp and 67 Nm of torque, ensuring swift acceleration and top-tier performance


Comfort and Design: With a compact, aerodynamic frame and adjustable suspension, the YZF-R7 is built for comfort and handling, offering superior control during high-speed rides​


Advanced Technology: The bike comes equipped with modern tech features, including fuel injection and a 6-speed transmission with assist and slipper clutch for smoother gear transitions​


Braking and Safety: Dual hydraulic disc brakes and ABS offer outstanding stopping power, while the robust suspension enhances overall stability and safety


Eco-Friendly Innovations: With a fuel efficiency of 58 MPG, the YZF-R7 strikes a balance between performance and fuel economy, adhering to Euro emission standards
