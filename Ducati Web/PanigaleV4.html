The Ultimate Racing Experience

the Panigale V4's unparalleled performance and engineering excellence, which bring MotoGP-level technology to the road. Designed for riders who demand pure adrenaline and precise control, the Panigale V4 stands as a symbol of Ducati's commitment to pushing the boundaries of speed and power. Whether on track or street, the Panigale V4 offers an unrivaled experience in the world of superbikes.


Pricing and Options

Estimated starting price for the Ducati Panigale V4: ₱1,500,000 to ₱1,600,000 (depending on optional upgrades and dealer pricing).

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 1,103cc V4, Desmodromic, liquid-cooled

Power Output: 224 hp (166 kW) @ 15,250 rpm

Torque: 112 Nm (82.6 lb-ft) @ 11,500 rpm

Configuration: Bosch electronic fuel injection, 52mm throttle bodies, and ride-by-wire technology for smooth and precise throttle control.

Valve System: Desmodromic valve system, designed for high-revving performance, minimizing valve float even at extremely high rpm.

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: While the Panigale V4 is more focused on performance, its efficiency is still relatively respectable for a machine of its caliber, thanks to lightweight components and an optimized powertrain.

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The Panigale V4 is powered by a 1103cc Desmosedici Stradale V4 engine, producing 214 hp at 13,000 rpm and 124 Nm of torque at 10,000 rpm.


Design and Aerodynamics: The racing-inspired design of the Panigale V4 is defined by sleek lines, an aggressive front end, and sculpted side panels that enhance its aerodynamic efficiency.


Advanced Technology: Equipped with Ducati Quick Shift (DQS) for seamless shifting and Ducati Traction Control (DTC EVO 2), providing superior traction and stability in every condition.


Braking and Safety: Electronic suspension adjustment (ESA) ensures adaptive and responsive handling on both road and track.


Limited Edition & Exclusivity: Panigale V4 Special Edition: For collectors and enthusiasts, the Panigale V4 Special Edition model offers additional carbon fiber components, a numbered plaque, and other enhancements for track-focused performance.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: The Panigale V4 is designed to balance performance and fuel efficiency, with improvements in fuel delivery systems contributing to a fuel efficiency of approximately 15 km/l on the road.
