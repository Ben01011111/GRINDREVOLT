Superleggera V4

The Ducati Superleggera V4 is the pinnacle of Ducati’s 
engineering, offering an exclusive, limited-edition superbike built 
for the most discerning riders. At its heart is a 1,103cc V4 engine 
that churns out a jaw-dropping 224 horsepower, making it one of the 
most powerful production motorcycles ever.

Superleggera V4

Pure Racing DNA, Limitless Performance

the Superleggera V4's design as a high-performance machine, rooted in Ducati's rich racing heritage, delivering unparalleled track-ready capabilities while remaining a street-legal, exclusive piece of machinery for ultimate riders.


Pricing and Options

The price for the Ducati Superleggera V4 is significantly higher than most superbikes, with an approximate starting price of ₱2,000,000 to ₱2,800,000, depending on the model year and dealer specifications.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 1,103cc V4, Desmodromic, liquid-cooled

Power Output: 224 hp (166 kW) @ 15,250 rpm

Torque: 112 Nm (82.6 lb-ft) @ 11,500 rpm

Configuration: Bosch electronic fuel injection, 52mm throttle bodies, and ride-by-wire technology for smooth and precise throttle control.

Valve System: Desmodromic valve system, designed for high-revving performance, minimizing valve float even at extremely high rpm.

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: The Superleggera V4 is optimized for performance rather than fuel economy, but its efficiency is augmented by a lightweight structure, giving it a competitive edge on the track.

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by a 998cc V4 engine, the Superleggera V4 delivers an incredible 234 hp at 15,500 rpm with a top-end torque of 119 Nm at 11,750 rpm.


Comfort and Design: The Superleggera V4 features extensive use of carbon fiber to minimize weight while maintaining rigidity, including carbon fiber swingarm, subframe, and fairings.


Advanced Technology: Equipped with the Ducati Quick Shift (DQS) EVO 2 system for fast gear shifts and Ducati Traction Control (DTC) EVO 2, ensuring precise control in various conditions.


Braking and Safety: The Öhlins NPX 25/30 forks and TTX 36 shock absorbers ensure incredible stability and precision, whether on the track or on twisty roads.


Limited Edition & Exclusivity: The Superleggera V4 is a limited edition bike, with only 500 units made worldwide, making it an exclusive model for collectors and enthusiasts.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: The Superleggera V4 is equipped with advanced fuel injection systems and BS6-compliant technology for cleaner emissions without compromising power.
