SuperSport 950

Sporty Spirit. Everyday Ride.

the SuperSport 950’s blend of high-performance sportbike characteristics with practicality for daily use, making it ideal for both exhilarating rides and everyday commuting.


Pricing and Options

the price for the SuperSport 950 typically ranges from ₱800,000 to ₱950,000, depending on the model and optional features.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 937cc L-Twin engine, liquid-cooled, with Desmodromic valve system.

Power Output: 110 hp (81 kW) at 9,000 rpm – providing excellent performance for both track days and street use.

Torque: 93 Nm (68 lb-ft) at 6,500 rpm – delivering solid mid-range power, ideal for spirited riding on winding roads.

Configuration: Bosch electronic fuel injection, 52mm throttle bodies, and ride-by-wire technology for smooth and precise throttle control.

Valve System: Desmodromic valve system – a hallmark of Ducati’s engineering, this system ensures precise valve control without the need for springs, offering reliability at high RPMs.

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Fuel efficiency of approximately 17-18 km/l (5.5-5.8 liters per 100 km), which strikes a balance between performance and economy for a sportbike of its class.

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by a 937cc L-Twin Testastretta 11° engine, delivering 110 hp (81 kW) at 9,000 rpm and 93 Nm (68 lb-ft) of torque at 6,500 rpm.


Comfort and Design: The SuperSport 950’s design is sleek and aggressive, with a sharp front end and dynamic bodywork.


Advanced Technology: Features riding modes (Sport, Touring, Urban) that adjust power delivery, traction control, and ABS settings..


Braking and Safety: Adjustable suspension (Showa forks and Sachs rear shock) provides a comfortable and stable ride, even in challenging road conditions.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: The SuperSport 950 meets Euro 5 emissions standards, ensuring lower emissions without compromising performance.
