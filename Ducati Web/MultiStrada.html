Engine Specification

Engine Type: 1158cc V4 Granturismo engine

Power Output: Approximately 170 horsepower at 10,500 rpm

Torque: Around 125 Nm at 8,750 rpm

Configuration: Liquid-cooled, four-cylinder V4

Valve System: Desmodromic valve-less system, reducing maintenance requirements

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Rear-cylinder deactivation technology reduces fuel consumption during light throttle or idle conditions

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The Multistrada V4 is powered by a 1158cc Granturismo V4 engine, producing 170 hp at 10,750 rpm and 124 Nm of torque at 8,750 rpm. This counter-rotating crankshaft engine provides a smooth, twin-pulse firing order for balanced performance and reliability


Comfort and Design: For 2025, Ducati has made several ergonomic improvements, including repositioning luggage to enhance passenger comfort. The design has also been refined with more painted bodywork and reduced black plastic for a sleeker appearance


Advanced Technology: The Multistrada V4 boasts features like adaptive cruise control, blind spot monitoring, forward collision warning, and the Ducati Vehicle Observer system, which enhances rider assist functions by simulating inputs from virtual sensors


Braking and Safety: Brembo brakes are standard across all models, with enhancements like a larger 280mm rear disc for better braking performance


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Cylinder deactivation technology now works both at a standstill and during low-speed movement, reducing heat and fuel consumption by approximately 6% compared to previous models


Adventure-Ready: The bike's high ground clearance, robust suspension, and Enduro riding mode ensure it can handle both paved roads and challenging off-road terrains
