Monster SP

The Ducati Monster SP is an iconic and thrilling naked bike, 
specifically crafted for riders seeking an adrenaline-pumping 
performance combined with style. As a standout in Ducati’s Monster 
lineup, the Monster SP is a step above the standard Monster, 
bringing more advanced features, enhanced aesthetics, 
and improved technology for a high-performance experience.

Monster SP

Unleash the Monster Within: Precision, Power, and Style.

the core attributes of the Monster SP — its precise handling, raw power, and distinctive style. Engineered for those who seek high performance on both the street and track, the Monster SP brings Ducati’s racing technology to everyday riders. With features like advanced suspension, Brembo brakes, and a robust 937cc engine, it embodies Ducati’s commitment to delivering thrill, adrenaline, and Italian design.


Pricing and Options

Estimated starting price: ₱910,000 to ₱1,100,000, depending on dealer pricing and optional upgrades

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 937cc, liquid-cooled L-Twin with Desmodromic valve system.

Power Output: 110 hp (81 kW) @ 9,250 rpm.

Torque: 92 Nm (68 lb-ft) at 6,500 rpm, delivering excellent low- and mid-range torque for quick acceleration and responsive handling.

Configuration: Bosch electronic fuel injection with 53 mm throttle bodies and ride-by-wire technology, for precise throttle control and smooth power delivery.

Valve System: Desmodromic valve-less system, which helps to reduce maintenance requirements while providing optimal performance.

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 17.9 km/l (5.6 liters per 100 km), showcasing a balance between power and fuel economy.

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by a 937cc Testastretta 11° twin-cylinder engine producing 111 hp at 9,250 rpm and 93 Nm torque at 6,500 rpm.


Comfort and Design: Lightweight aluminum alloy frame and ergonomic enhancements prioritize rider comfort and handling.


Advanced Technology: Equipped with ABS Cornering, Ducati Traction Control, Ducati Wheelie Control, and Launch Control for enhanced safety and performance.


Braking and Safety: Features Brembo Stylema® front calipers paired with Öhlins NIX30 suspension for top-tier braking and handling.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Improved fuel efficiency of 18.87 km/l, ideal for sporty yet economical riding
