Engine Specification

Engine Type: 1,103cc Desmodromic V4 engine.

Power Output: 208 hp (154 kW) @ 12,750 rpm

Torque: 123 Nm (90.6 lb-ft) @ 11,500 rpm

Configuration: Bosch electronic fuel injection, 52mm throttle bodies, and ride-by-wire technology for smooth and precise throttle control.

Valve System: Desmodromic valve system for high-revving performance and precision, preventing valve float at extreme rpm

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by a 1,103cc V4 engine, producing 208 hp at 13,000 rpm and 123 Nm of torque at 9,500 rpm.


Comfort and Design: The aggressive design of the Streetfighter V4 emphasizes its naked bike look, with minimalistic bodywork and striking, angular features.


Advanced Technology: It comes with riding modes (Sport, Road, Wet, and Race) which adjust performance parameters like power delivery, suspension settings, and electronic controls for different riding conditions.


Braking and Safety: The Brembo Stylema® brakes ensure exceptional stopping power, combined with Öhlins suspension for superior handling.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: The Streetfighter V4 is designed to meet Euro 5 emissions standards, ensuring reduced environmental impact while still delivering maximum performance.
