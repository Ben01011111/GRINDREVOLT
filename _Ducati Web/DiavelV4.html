The Spirit of Pure Fun

The Ducati Hypermotard 950 embodies the thrill of spirited rides and the joy of motorcycling. Its tagline, "Live. Play. Ride.", perfectly captures the essence of the bike—a blend of playfulness, performance, and practicality.


Pricing and Options

In the Philippines, the Ducati Hypermotard 950 starts around ₱1.0 million to ₱1.2 million, depending on variants and dealer location

Engine Specification

Engine Type: Testastretta 11° L-Twin, liquid-cooled

Power Output: Approximately 114 horsepower at 9,000 rpm with 937 cc

Torque: Around 96 Nm at 7,250 rpm

Configuration: Electronic fuel injection with a 53mm throttle body4

Valve System: Desmodromic, 4 valves per cylinder

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 20 km/l, depending on riding conditions

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The Hypermotard 950 is powered by a 937cc Testastretta 11° V-twin engine. It produces 114 hp at 9,000 rpm and 96 Nm of torque at 7,250 rpm. Its quick-revving engine offers robust low-to-mid-range power delivery, making it ideal for spirited rides.


Comfort and Design: The bike features a minimalist, aggressive supermoto-inspired design with a high-mounted twin exhaust and sharp aesthetics. Its upright riding position and well-padded seat enhance comfort for both urban commutes and sporty rides.


Advanced Technology: Equipped with advanced electronics, the Hypermotard includes a 6-axis IMU, Bosch Cornering ABS, Ducati Traction Control (DTC), Ducati Wheelie Control (DWC), and three customizable riding modes (Sport, Touring, Urban).


Braking and Safety: Featuring fully adjustable Marzocchi suspension (or Ohlins in the SP variant) and a trellis frame, the bike is lightweight and agile, ensuring precise handling and cornering performance.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: While prioritizing performance, the Hypermotard 950 maintains compliance with Euro 5 emissions standards, ensuring an eco-conscious ride without compromising on power.
