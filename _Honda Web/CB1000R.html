CB1000R

The Honda CB1000R is a premium naked sports bike, known for its unique blend 
of modern styling and raw performance. Designed with a minimalist Neo Sports Café aesthetic, 
it combines aggressive streetfighter looks with refined craftsmanship, making it suitable for 
both city commuting and spirited rides.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 998cc liquid-cooled, inline-four engine.

Power Output: 143.5 hp (107 kW) @ 10,500 rpm.

Torque: 104 Nm (76.7 lb-ft) @ 8,250 rpm.

Configuration: DOHC (Double Overhead Camshaft), four valves per cylinder.

Valve System: Four-valve per cylinder layout with precise camshaft timing for optimal performance.

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Estimated around 17–18 km/l, offering a balance of power and economy.

Key Features:
