X-ADV

The Honda X-ADV is a unique scooter designed to offer 
the best of both worlds: urban mobility and off-road exploration. 
This hybrid machine combines the comfort and ease of a scooter with 
the ruggedness of an adventure bike, making it perfect for riders who want versatility.

X-ADV

Adventure in Every Ride.

The X-ADV combines rugged off-road capability with a comfortable scooter design, making it ideal for riders who want both urban commuting and off-road exploration. It features a unique dual-clutch transmission (DCT) that offers both automatic and manual shifting, enhancing the overall riding experience.


Pricing and Options

The price of the Honda X-ADV in the Philippines typically ranges from ₱600,000 to ₱700,000, depending on the dealer and any optional accessories or upgrades.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 745cc Parallel Twin, liquid-cooled, SOHC

Power Output: 58 hp (43 kW) @ 6,250 rpm

Torque: 69 Nm @ 4,750 rpm

Configuration: Electronic fuel injection with 41mm throttle bodies

Valve System: SOHC (Single Overhead Cam), 8 valves

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approx. 24 km/l

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Smooth power delivery ideal for both city commuting and light off-roading.


Comfort and Design: Adventure bike-inspired styling with a modern, rugged look.


Advanced Technology: Multiple riding modes (Sport, Rain, and Standard) that adjust the bike’s performance to suit road conditions.


Versatility: With its 17-inch front and 15-inch rear wheels, the X-ADV handles both road and mild off-road conditions.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: The engine is designed to meet Euro 4 emissions standards, ensuring it runs efficiently while being environmentally friendly.


Suspension & Handling: 41mm front forks and twin rear shock absorbers enhance its off-road capability while maintaining comfort on paved roads.
