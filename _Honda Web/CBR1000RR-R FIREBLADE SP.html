Engine Specification

Engine Type: 999cc, liquid-cooled, inline-4 engine

Power Output: 214 hp (160 kW) @ 14,500 rpm

Torque: 113 Nm (83.4 lb-ft) @ 12,500 rpm

Configuration: Fuel-injected with a 6-speed transmission and a quick-shifter for seamless gear shifts.

Valve System: DOHC (Double Overhead Cam) with 16 valves

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 13-15 km/l (varies based on riding conditions), with the focus being more on power output and handling than fuel economy.

Key Features:

High-Performance Engine: Powered by a 999cc inline-4 engine, the CBR1000RR-R Fireblade SP delivers 214 hp at 14,500 rpm and 113 Nm of torque at 12,500 rpm, ensuring exceptional power for both track and road use.


Comfort and Design: The CBR1000RR-R Fireblade SP features an aggressive aerodynamic design, including aerodynamic wings that increase downforce for better stability at high speeds.


Advanced Technology: Equipped with Honda Selectable Torque Control (HSTC) and Wheelie Control, the Fireblade SP offers enhanced rider control, especially in challenging conditions.


Braking and Safety: It features Brembo Stylema brakes for exceptional stopping power, providing maximum safety and control in high-speed scenarios.


Optimized for Performance:: With features like launch control, traction control, and electronic throttle mapping, the CBR1000RR-R Fireblade SP ensures maximum performance for competitive racing and everyday riding alike.


Track-Oriented Design: The bike is built with full carbon fiber bodywork, reducing weight and enhancing aerodynamics, further solidifying its track-ready capabilities.
