Engine Specification

Engine Type: 895cc parallel-twin engine.

Power Output: A99 hp (73 kW) at 8,500 rpm.

Torque: 92 Nm (67.8 lb-ft) at 6,500 rpm.

Configuration: The engine features Bosch electronic fuel injection, providing consistent fuel delivery for better performance.

Valve System: The engine uses a DOHC (Dual Overhead Camshaft) valve system, allowing for precise control of the intake and exhaust valves

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: The F 900 XR is efficient with an approximate fuel consumption of 4.4 liters per 100 km (22.7 km/l).

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powerful and efficient engine tailored for sport-touring adventures.


Comfort and Design: Sleek and dynamic styling blends adventure-ready looks with sportbike aesthetics.


Advanced Technology: Equipped with a 6.5-inch TFT display featuring BMW Motorrad Connectivity for navigation, calls, and entertainment.


Braking and Safety: Traction Control (DTC) and cornering ABS for enhanced safety and control.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Balanced fuel efficiency, with an estimated mileage of 20 km/l.
