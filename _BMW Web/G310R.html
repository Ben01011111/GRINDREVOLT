Pricing and Options

The price of the BMW G 310 R in the Philippines typically ranges from ₱260,000 to ₱300,000, depending on the model year, dealer pricing, and any additional features or accessories.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 313cc, single-cylinder, liquid-cooled, DOHC (Dual Overhead Camshaft) engine.

Power Output: 34 hp (25 kW) at 9,500 rpm.

Torque: 28 Nm (20.6 lb-ft) at 7,500 rpm.

Configuration: Bosch electronic fuel injection, enhancing throttle response and overall fuel efficiency.

Valve System: DOHC with 4 valves. This allows the engine to achieve better high-revving performance and efficiency while reducing maintenance demands typical of other valve systems.

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Around 30 km/l (2.6 liters per 100 km), offering a good balance of fuel economy and performance for a lightweight bike.

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by a 313cc single-cylinder, liquid-cooled engine, producing 34 hp (25 kW) at 9,500 rpm and 28 Nm torque at 7,500 rpm.


Comfort and Design: The G 310 R features a compact, sporty design, with sharp lines and an aggressive stance. The styling is inspired by BMW's larger roadster models, like the S 1000 R, but in a smaller, more agile package.


Advanced Technology: TFT display provides clear information on speed, fuel level, and ride modes.


Braking and Safety: Equipped with Brembo braking system with a 300mm front disc and 240mm rear disc, ensuring reliable stopping power.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Efficient fuel consumption of approximately 30 km/l, ideal for daily commuting and long rides.
