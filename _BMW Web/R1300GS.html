R 1300 GS

The BMW R1300 GS is the latest iteration of the iconic GS series, 
designed to tackle long-distance adventures and off-road challenges with ease. 
It boasts a 1,300cc twin-cylinder boxer engine, offering 145 hp and 110 lb-ft of torque, 
delivering exceptional power across diverse terrains.

the R1300 GS's ability to conquer both on-road and off-road terrain, offering adventure-seekers the freedom to explore the world without limits. It highlights the bike's combination of power, technology, and comfort, making it an ideal choice for those ready for any journey, no matter the environment.


Pricing and Options

the 2024 BMW R1300 GS is priced between PHP 1,675,000 and PHP 1,855,000, depending on the variant.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 1,300cc twin-cylinder boxer engine

Power Output: 145 hp

Torque: 110 lb-ft

Configuration: Horizontal opposed (boxer) twin-cylinder engine

Valve System: ShiftCam technology (variable valve timing)

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 51.2 mpg

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: 145 hp of peak power and 110 lb-ft of torque, making it ideal for a variety of terrains, from highways to off-road trails.


Comfort and Design: Aerodynamic design, optimized for stability at high speeds and during off-road use.


Advanced Technology: Riding Modes allow riders to select the best settings for different conditions: Road, Rain, Dynamic, and Enduro modes.


Braking and Safety: BMW's Intelligent Emergency Call System for added safety during emergencies, notifying the authorities if the rider is in distress.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: The R1300 GS delivers excellent fuel efficiency, achieving around 51.2 mpg due to the efficient design of its boxer engine and ShiftCam technology.


Off-Road Capability: Enduro Pro mode allows the rider to access advanced off-road functionality.
