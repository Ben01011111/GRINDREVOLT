S 1000 R

The BMW S 1000 R is a dynamic roadster with superbike DNA. It features a high-performance 999cc inline 
four-cylinder engine that delivers 165 hp at 11,000 rpm and 114 Nm of torque at 9,250 rpm. 
The bike's lightweight design, particularly with the M package, brings its total weight to just 199 kg fully fueled. 
This helps ensure agility and precise handling.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 999cc, 4-stroke, inline 4-cylinder engine.

Power Output: 165 hp (121 kW) at 11,000 rpm.

Torque: 114 Nm (84 lb-ft) at 9,250 rpm.

Configuration: Electronic fuel injection with ride-by-wire throttle, integrated with advanced engine management system.

Valve System: DOHC (Dual Overhead Camshaft), 16 valves, designed for high-performance output at high revs.

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 15-17 km/l, depending on driving conditions and use​

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The S 1000 R is powered by a 999cc inline-4 engine, producing 165 hp and 114 Nm of torque, offering impressive acceleration and top-end performance.


Comfort and Design: The aggressive design emphasizes its performance-oriented character, with a minimalist approach to rider ergonomics, making it comfortable for both short rides and longer journeys.


Advanced Technology: Equipped with BMW’s Dynamic Traction Control (DTC) and BMW Motorrad’s Race ABS, ensuring enhanced safety, stability, and confidence, especially in challenging conditions.


Braking and Safety: It features Brembo brakes and Michelin Power Cup tires for superior stopping power and grip on the road.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Although built for performance, the S 1000 R offers an economical fuel efficiency considering its power output, contributing to extended riding range.
