S 1000 RR

The BMW S1000RR is a premium superbike that pushes the boundaries of performance with its 999cc in-line 4-cylinder engine, 
which features the innovative BMW Shift Cam system. This engine produces a thrilling 205 horsepower at 13,750 rpm 
and 83 lb-ft of torque at 11,000 rpm.

Pricing and Options

The BMW S1000RR in the Philippines is priced between PHP 1.575 million and PHP 2.995 million, depending on the variant and additional features.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 999cc, Liquid-cooled, Inline 4-cylinder engine.

Power Output: 205 hp (153 kW) at 13,750 rpm.

Torque: 83 lb-ft (113 Nm) at 11,000 rpm.

Configuration: The S1000RR uses Bosch electronic fuel injection, a 52 mm throttle body, and ride-by-wire technology to ensure smooth and precise throttle response.

Valve System: The bike features a Desmodromic valve system, known for its precise control over valve timing, enhancing performance and reducing maintenance needs.

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Despite its high performance, the S1000RR is fairly efficient, offering around 16-18 km/L (depending on riding conditions), a result of its optimized engine and lightweight design.

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: ShiftCam technology ensures optimal performance by adjusting intake valve timing, improving both low-end torque and high-rev power for a smoother, more dynamic ride.


Comfort and Design: The lightweight frame and aggressive aerodynamic design enhance agility and handling, making the S1000RR track-ready.


Advanced Technology: Equipped with Dynamic Traction Control (DTC), Anti-lock Braking System (ABS Pro), and BMW Motorrad Race ABS for superior handling and safety in all riding conditions.


Braking and Safety: Brembo Stylema® calipers provide exceptional braking power, ensuring quick stopping even at high speeds.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Despite its high-performance engine, the S1000RR has been optimized for better fuel efficiency, with improvements in fuel injection technology that offer balanced power delivery for fuel economy during everyday use.
