R 1250 GS Adventure

The BMW R1250 GS Adventure is designed to handle challenging terrains while providing 
a luxurious riding experience. This adventure motorcycle combines power, stability, 
and comfort for long-distance rides, whether on paved roads or off-road conditions.

Engineered for Exploration. Built for Adventure.

the R1250 GS Adventure’s rugged durability and its design to excel in both challenging terrains and long-distance touring, emphasizing its dual nature of reliability and adventure-ready performance.


Pricing and Options

The price of the BMW R1250 GS Adventure starts from around ₱1,400,000 but may vary depending on the dealer and additional configurations.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 1,254cc, Boxer Twin, air/liquid-cooled engine.

Power Output: 136 hp (100 kW) at 7,750 rpm.

Torque: 143 Nm (105 lb-ft) at 6,250 rpm.

Configuration: Dual overhead cam (DOHC), featuring BMW's famous Boxer engine configuration with shaft drive.

Valve System: BMW's ShiftCam technology, which adjusts the timing of the intake valves for better fuel efficiency, more power, and smoother running at different RPM levels.

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Estimated around 5.3 to 5.8 L/100 km (47-52 mpg), depending on riding conditions.

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by a 1254cc Boxer twin engine, producing 136 hp and 143 Nm of torque.


Comfort and Design: The R1250 GS Adventure offers long suspension travel, adjustable ergonomics, and a large fuel tank (30 liters) to ensure comfort and convenience for long trips.


Advanced Technology: Features Dynamic ESA (Electronic Suspension Adjustment) for on-the-fly suspension adjustments based on load and riding conditions.


Braking and Safety: BMW Integral ABS Pro and hill start control ensure better safety and stability.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Compliant with Euro 5 emissions regulations and engineered for better fuel economy, especially on long rides.
