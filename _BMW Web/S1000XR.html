Pricing and Options

The 2024 BMW S1000XR is priced between ₱1,455,000 and ₱1,855,000 depending on the variant, with the base model starting at ₱1.455 million.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 999cc, inline-four, liquid-cooled

Power Output: 165 horsepower (123 kW) at 11,000 rpm

Torque: 114 Nm at 9,250 rpm

Configuration: Bosch electronic fuel injection, with ride-by-wire technology for precise throttle control

Valve System: DOHC (Double Overhead Camshaft), 16 valves, providing high-revving performance and efficient valve actuation

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 18-20 km/l, depending on riding conditions and mode, offering a balance between performance and long-distance touring capability.

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by a 999cc inline-4 engine, producing 165 hp at 11,000 rpm and 114 Nm of torque at 9,250 rpm.


Comfort and Design: Equipped with a semi-active suspension system (Dynamic ESA) for comfort and handling adjustments.


Advanced Technology: Includes dynamic traction control, ABS Pro, and riding modes (Rain, Road, Dynamic, and Race Pro) for an adaptable and safe riding experience.


Braking and Safety: Features Brembo brakes with advanced anti-lock braking system (ABS) for reliable stopping power in all conditions.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: With a fuel tank capacity of 20 liters, the S1000XR offers an impressive range, ideal for long-distance touring and sports riding. Fuel efficiency stands at around 18 km/l under normal conditions.
