NINJA H2R

Unleashing Power, Mastering Speed

The Kawasaki Ninja H2R is the epitome of engineering excellence, designed for ultimate performance. With its supercharged 998cc engine, this machine offers unrivaled speed and handling on the track. Whether you're looking to set records or simply enjoy a thrilling ride, the Ninja H2R combines raw power with aerodynamic design, creating a motorcycle that’s in a class of its own.


Pricing and Options

The Kawasaki Ninja H2R is a track-only model, and it is priced at approximately PHP 1,700,000 - PHP 2,000,000, depending on the dealer and location. This price reflects the exclusivity, advanced technology, and performance of the motorcycle.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: Supercharged Inline-4

Power Output: 310 hp (at 14,000 rpm)

Torque: 165 Nm (at 12,500 rpm)

Configuration: Inline-4, DOHC

Valve System: 16-Valve, DOHC (Double Overhead Camshaft)

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 10-12 km/l (varies with usage)

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The Ninja H2R is powered by a supercharged 998cc inline-4 engine that produces an awe-inspiring 310 horsepower and 165 Nm of torque. This results in blistering acceleration and top-tier performance. Its advanced supercharger system ensures maximum efficiency and performance, even at higher altitudes.


Comfort and Design: While performance takes the spotlight, the design of the H2R doesn't fall short. The aggressive aerodynamic bodywork and carefully crafted chassis provide exceptional stability at high speeds, while the ergonomics are geared towards the racer’s body, ensuring comfort during intense rides.


Advanced Technology: Packed with cutting-edge technology, the Ninja H2R features Kawasaki's proprietary Electronic Control Unit (ECU) that fine-tunes the engine’s performance. Additionally, the motorcycle is equipped with sophisticated traction control, quick shifter, and a unique supercharger system for unparalleled acceleration.


Braking and Safety: The H2R comes with top-of-the-line braking components, including dual 330mm front discs paired with Brembo M50 calipers, ensuring superior stopping power. The safety features include advanced cornering management systems, traction control, and race-level suspension for stability and precision under extreme conditions.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: While designed for raw power, the Ninja H2R's supercharged engine is also optimized for fuel efficiency, considering its performance class. The integration of lightweight materials further contributes to a more efficient performance, ensuring that even a beast of this caliber remains environmentally conscious.
