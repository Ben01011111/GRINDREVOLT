Z650RS

The Kawasaki Z650RS is a perfect fusion of retro charm and modern performance, 
designed for riders who seek the best of both worlds. Drawing inspiration from 
Kawasaki’s iconic Z series, the Z650RS brings a classic aesthetic to a 
contemporary motorcycle, making it a standout on the streets.

VERSYS 650

Retro Style, Modern Performance

The Kawasaki Z650RS blends classic retro aesthetics with modern engineering, delivering a seamless experience of agility, performance, and nostalgic flair. Whether you're cruising city streets or embarking on scenic rides, the Z650RS is designed to excite with its smooth handling and responsive engine.


Pricing and Options

The Kawasaki Z650RS is priced at approximately ₱450,000 to ₱500,000 depending on location, dealer, and available promotions.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 4-stroke, parallel twin

Power Output: 68 hp (50.2 kW) @ 8,000 rpm

Torque: 64 Nm @ 6,700 rpm

Configuration: Parallel twin, liquid-cooled

Valve System: DOHC (Dual Overhead Cam), 8 valves

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 20-25 km/l (depending on riding conditions)

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by a liquid-cooled, 649cc parallel-twin engine, the Z650RS provides an ideal balance of power and responsiveness. With 68 hp and 64 Nm of torque, it is perfectly suited for both city commuting and spirited weekend rides. The engine is tuned for smooth delivery across the rev range, making it accessible for newer riders yet thrilling for seasoned enthusiasts.


Comfort and Design: The Z650RS offers a comfortable upright riding position with a carefully crafted retro design. Its well-padded seat and relaxed ergonomics ensure comfort during long rides, while the classic round headlamp, vintage-inspired tank, and minimalistic bodywork reflect the essence of Kawasaki's heritage. The attention to detail in the design makes it a true head-turner.


Advanced Technology: Equipped with modern technology, the Z650RS features a digital/analog display, LED lighting for better visibility, and a responsive throttle system. It also comes with Kawasaki’s KTRC (Kawasaki Traction Control) system for enhanced stability, and the assist and slipper clutch provides smoother gear shifting, especially under aggressive riding conditions.


Braking and Safety: The Z650RS is fitted with dual front discs and a rear disc for reliable stopping power, with dual-channel ABS for added safety. The well-balanced braking system ensures confident stopping performance in various road conditions. The bike's lightweight frame and responsive suspension further enhance its agility and stability.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: The Z650RS is designed to meet stringent emission standards with efficient fuel management and a lightweight chassis. Its fuel-efficient engine contributes to lower emissions while offering a fun and eco-friendly ride. It’s an excellent option for those seeking a balance between performance and sustainability.
