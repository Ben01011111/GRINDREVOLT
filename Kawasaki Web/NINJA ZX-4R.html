NINJA ZX-4R

Unleash Pure Performance with the Ninja ZX-4R: A Thrilling 400cc Revolution.

The Kawasaki Ninja ZX-4R redefines the 400cc segment with a high-revving, powerful in-line four engine, delivering exhilarating performance and a truly thrilling ride. With advanced technology, agile handling, and a sleek design, it's built for those who crave top-tier performance in a compact package.

Engine Specification

Engine Type: Liquid-cooled, 4-stroke, DOHC 16-valve in-line four

Power Output: 77 PS (76.43 hp) @ 14,000 rpm

Torque: 39.6 Nm @ 13,200 rpm

Configuration: In-line four

Valve System: DOHC, 16-valves

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approx. 17.5 km/l (real-world average), though this can vary based on riding style

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by a liquid-cooled 399cc in-line four engine, the ZX-4R produces 77 PS at 14,000 rpm, making it one of the most powerful bikes in its class. The engine is coupled with a 6-speed gearbox and advanced fuel injection for smooth, responsive performance.


Comfort and Design: The bike features a sporty, ergonomic design with an 800mm seat height, ideal for a variety of riders. The trellis frame ensures lightweight agility, while the inverted front forks and gas-charged rear suspension offer superior handling and comfort.


Advanced Technology: Equipped with a full-color TFT display, the Ninja ZX-4R offers multiple riding modes, integrated smartphone notifications, and real-time fuel economy tracking. Advanced tech like KTRC (Kawasaki Traction Control) and Power Modes enhances safety and performance.


Braking and Safety: Dual semi-floating 290mm front discs with 4-piston calipers, and a single 220mm rear disc, equipped with ABS, ensure exceptional braking power. The bike's safety features also include a riding mode selector, which adjusts power delivery based on conditions.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: The ZX-4R's efficient fuel system and improved combustion technology enhance fuel efficiency, promoting eco-friendliness without compromising performance.
