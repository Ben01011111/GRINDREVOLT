VERSYS 1100 S

Versys 1100 S: Unleash Adventure in Comfort

With its impressive power, innovative technology, and superior comfort, the Versys 1100 S is perfect for riders seeking to enjoy both exhilarating performance and long-distance touring comfort.


Pricing and Options

The Kawasaki Versys 1100 S is priced at around ₱799,000 in the Philippines​

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 1,099cc, Liquid-cooled, 4-stroke, DOHC, inline 4-cylinder

Power Output: 135 PS (approx. 133 hp) at 9,500 rpm

Torque: 102 Nm at 7,500 rpm

Configuration: Fuel injection with dual throttle valves

Valve System: DOHC, 16 valves

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: 5.6 L/100 km (estimated)

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by a 1,099cc inline-four engine, the Versys 1100 S boasts 135 PS, offering strong torque across all RPM ranges for smooth acceleration and long-distance comfort. The bike is equipped with a Kawasaki Quick Shifter (KQS) for seamless gear changes, improving the riding experience


Comfort and Design: Featuring a comfortable upright riding position and plush seats with thick urethane cushioning, the Versys 1100 S ensures a relaxing ride. It also comes with adjustable wind protection for added comfort on long journeys


Advanced Technology: The bike is packed with electronic cruise control, IMU-enhanced electronics, and smartphone connectivity. It also offers a TFT display for easy readability, while LED cornering lights enhance visibility​


Braking and Safety: Equipped with dual-channel ABS and traction control, the Versys 1100 S provides excellent handling and stability in various riding conditions​


Eco-Friendly Innovations: With an efficient fuel consumption of 5.6 L/100 km, the Versys 1100 S is designed to reduce fuel consumption, making it an eco-friendly choice for touring​
