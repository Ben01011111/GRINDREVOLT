VERSYS 650

Versatility Unleashed.

The Kawasaki Versys 650 combines power, comfort, and cutting-edge technology to provide an unbeatable adventure touring experience. Whether navigating the city or cruising through challenging terrains, the Versys ensures a smooth, enjoyable ride that adapts to every need.


Pricing and Options

The Kawasaki Versys 650 is priced at approximately ₱499,000 in the Philippines​

Engine Specification

Engine Type: Liquid-cooled, Parallel Twin, Fuel Injected

Power Output: 69.3 hp @ 8,500 rpm

Torque: 61 Nm @ 7,000 rpm

Configuration: Parallel Twin

Valve System: DOHC (Double Overhead Cam)

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approx. 20-25 km/L (depending on riding conditions)

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by a 649cc parallel twin engine, the Versys 650 delivers 69.3 horsepower and 61 Nm of torque, providing excellent performance across varied terrains. It is equipped with a 6-speed transmission, offering smooth and responsive gear shifts for both urban and off-road adventures.


Comfort and Design: The upright seating position, long-travel suspension, and adjustable windscreen ensure rider comfort on long journeys. A 21L fuel tank minimizes refueling stops, and its lightweight 218 kg body enhances agility while maintaining stability.


Advanced Technology: Featuring a fuel-injection system, the Versys 650 optimizes fuel efficiency and provides smooth throttle response. A remote preload adjuster on the rear suspension lets you easily tailor the bike's setup to suit different loads and riding conditions.


Braking and Safety: The Versys 650 is equipped with dual disc brakes and an anti-lock braking system (ABS), ensuring secure stopping power and stability even at higher speeds.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: The Versys 650's fuel-efficient engine ensures better mileage, contributing to lower emissions while offering the power and torque needed for both daily commutes and off-road exploration.
