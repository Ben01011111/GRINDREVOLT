YZF-R1M

Precision Performance, Unmatched Power.

The Yamaha YZF-R1M is designed for those who demand the utmost in performance and technology. With a powerful 200 hp engine, this superbike delivers extraordinary acceleration and speed. Enhanced with advanced electronics, it provides riders with unrivaled precision in handling and control. Ideal for both track enthusiasts and road warriors, the YZF-R1M is the epitome of racing innovation, providing the perfect balance of power, agility, and rider-focused tech.


Pricing and Options

The price for the Yamaha YZF-R1M typically ranges between ₱1,600,000 to ₱1,800,000 in the Philippines

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 4-stroke, liquid-cooled, inline 4

Power Output: 200 hp (150 kW) @ 13,500 rpm

Torque: 113.3 Nm @ 11,500 rpm

Configuration: Inline-4

Valve System: DOHC, 16 valves

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 15-18 km/l (varies with riding conditions)

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The YZF-R1M boasts a 998cc inline-4 engine that produces 200 hp, providing exceptional power and acceleration. Its crossplane crankshaft technology ensures smoother power delivery and better control during high-speed riding.


Comfort and Design: A racing-focused ergonomics layout ensures comfort during aggressive riding, while the lightweight, aerodynamic body design enhances both speed and stability. The adjustable suspension ensures that you can fine-tune comfort for your riding style.


Advanced Technology: The R1M features advanced electronics, including a highly refined Yamaha Ride Control (YRC) system, which incorporates features such as quick-shifter, traction control, cornering ABS, and a sophisticated electronic control system, allowing riders to push their limits with confidence.


Braking and Safety: Equipped with Brembo brakes and an advanced ABS system, the YZF-R1M delivers exceptional stopping power and safety. The front and rear brakes work seamlessly with the electronic stability control systems to keep the rider safe even in extreme conditions.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Yamaha’s fuel-efficient engine, coupled with their focus on emission standards, makes the YZF-R1M a more environmentally conscious option compared to other superbikes. Its fuel efficiency also ensures longer rides with fewer stops for refueling.
