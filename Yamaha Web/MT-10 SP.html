MT-10 SP

Unleash the Beast: Precision, Power, and Performance

The Yamaha MT-10SP is the ultimate street machine, blending brutal power with refined precision. Powered by a 998cc inline-4 engine that delivers 158 horsepower and 111Nm of torque, this hyper naked bike is engineered for those who crave exhilaration on every twist of the throttle.


Pricing and Options

The Yamaha MT-10 SP is priced at approximately ₱1,099,000 in the Philippines

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 4-stroke, liquid-cooled, DOHC, inline 4-cylinder

Power Output: 158.2 hp (117.0 kW) @ 11,500 rpm

Torque: 111.0 Nm (81.9 lb-ft) @ 9,000 rpm

Configuration: Inline 4

Valve System: DOHC, 16 valves

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 14-16 km/L (33-37 mpg)

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The MT-10SP is powered by a 998cc inline-4 engine, delivering 158.2 hp and 111 Nm of torque. This powerhouse allows for blistering acceleration and top-tier performance, ensuring you feel the rush of pure power whether on the streets or the track.


Comfort and Design: The MT-10SP features a refined design, with ergonomic rider positioning that maximizes comfort without compromising on aggressive styling. Its lightweight, compact frame, and adjustable suspension (Öhlins electronic) ensure a smooth ride, even on rough terrains, making it ideal for both long rides and spirited cornering.


Advanced Technology: Equipped with cutting-edge electronics, the MT-10SP includes a comprehensive suite of features like a fully-adjustable quickshifter, ride modes, and a color TFT display. It also boasts Yamaha’s D-Mode system, which allows riders to customize throttle response, offering a tailor-made riding experience.


Braking and Safety: The MT-10SP comes with top-tier braking performance, featuring a pair of 320mm front discs and radial-mount calipers. Combined with the advanced Yamaha R1-derived ABS system and Traction Control, these features enhance both handling and safety in all conditions.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: Despite its power, the MT-10SP incorporates eco-conscious technologies, such as the advanced fuel injection system, which optimizes fuel consumption and reduces emissions, ensuring better fuel efficiency and a reduced carbon footprint without sacrificing performance.
