TRACER 9 GT

The Ultimate Touring Experience.

The Yamaha Tracer 9 GT is designed for long-distance touring with powerful performance, refined comfort, and advanced technology, all wrapped in a stylish and practical package. Perfect for those who crave adventure without sacrificing comfort and control.


Pricing and Options

The Yamaha Tracer 9 GT is priced at approximately ₱929,000 in the Philippines

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 890cc liquid-cooled, DOHC inline 3-cylinder with 4 valves per cylinder

Power Output: 117 hp @ 10,000 rpm

Torque: 93 Nm @ 8,500 rpm

Configuration: Inline 3-cylinder

Valve System: DOHC, 4 valves per cylinder

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approx. 49 MPG (21 km/l)

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: Powered by a 890cc, 3-cylinder engine delivering 117 hp and 93 Nm of torque, the Tracer 9 GT ensures exceptional acceleration, seamless power delivery, and a thrilling ride across any terrain


Comfort and Design: With a fully adjustable KYB suspension, heated hand grips, and a comfortable two-position seat, this bike is built for riders who seek both performance and comfort on long tours​. It also features a hand-adjustable windshield and enhanced LED lighting for improved night visibility


Advanced Technology: Equipped with a color TFT display, three riding modes, Quick Shift System (QSS), and Chip-Controlled Throttle, the Tracer 9 GT allows riders to fine-tune their ride for optimal performance


Braking and Safety: Dual front discs and a rear disc brake with ABS provide confident stopping power, while the advanced traction control and cruise control systems enhance safety during long rides


Eco-Friendly Innovations: With its fuel-efficient engine and eco-conscious design, the Tracer 9 GT is designed to deliver excellent fuel economy, helping reduce environmental impact without compromising performance
