SUPER TÉNÉRÉ ES

The Ultimate Adventure Companion

The Yamaha Super Ténéré ES is built for those who demand a bike that can handle the toughest terrains while offering comfort and advanced features. Its 1,199cc engine delivers exceptional power and torque, making it ideal for long-distance touring and off-road exploration.


Pricing and Options

The Yamaha Super Ténéré ES is priced at ₱999,000 in the Philippines

Engine Specification

Engine Type: 4-stroke, parallel twin, liquid-cooled

Power Output: 112 hp (83.6 kW) at 7,250 rpm

Torque: 83 lb-ft (113 Nm) at 6,000 rpm

Configuration: Parallel Twin

Valve System: DOHC, 8-valve

Fuel Efficiency Enhancements: Approximately 45-50 mpg (5.2–4.7 L/100 km) depending on riding conditions

Key Features:

Engine and Performance: The Super Ténéré ES features a powerful 1,199cc parallel twin engine that offers exceptional power and torque, optimized for both on-road and off-road performance. The electronic throttle valve (YCC-T) and advanced fuel injection ensure smooth throttle response and efficient power delivery across various terrains.


Comfort and Design: The Super Ténéré ES is equipped with an electronically adjustable suspension (Yamaha's ES system) for a smooth, custom ride. The long-travel suspension offers superior comfort and handling on uneven surfaces, while the adjustable windscreen and ergonomically designed seat provide all-day comfort for long-distance touring.


Advanced Technology: The bike is loaded with Yamaha’s advanced technology, including traction control, ABS, cruise control, and ride modes that adjust the engine's power delivery to match riding conditions. The Super Ténéré ES is also equipped with a TFT display that provides essential information at a glance, even in direct sunlight.


Braking and Safety: Featuring dual 310mm front discs and a single 282mm rear disc, combined with a sophisticated ABS system, the Super Ténéré ES delivers excellent stopping power on both wet and dry surfaces. The bike’s traction control system ensures stability on loose gravel and mud, while its durable braking system provides confidence on any road.


Eco-Friendly Innovations: The Super Ténéré ES is designed to meet stringent environmental standards while maintaining top-tier performance. With its fuel-efficient engine and low emissions, it ensures that you can explore the world responsibly, offering both performance and sustainability.
